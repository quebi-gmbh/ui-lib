# Stepper

Two variants: **admin** (for editors and multi-step forms) and **kiosk** (for
the matching wizard footer).

## Admin

<Canvas of={StepperStories.Admin} />

- 32px bullets with labels.
- States: `upcoming` (ink-100 bg), `done` (brand-500 white), `active` (surface +
  brand-500 border with a 4px brand-100 ring).
- The line **after** a done step turns `brand-300` to read as progress fill.

## Kiosk

<Canvas of={StepperStories.Kiosk} />

- 28px bullets, no labels.
- Done shows `✓`; active keeps the step number with the brand ring.
- Lives in the kiosk footer on every wizard step.
- **Never auto-advance a kiosk wizard** — the customer must tap Continue.
