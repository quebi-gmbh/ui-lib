# Badge

Pill-shaped indicator, 12px `font-semibold`, `px-2 py-[3px]`. Every intent
pairs a 100-weight background with a matching 700/600-weight text.

## Variants

<Canvas of={BadgeStories.Variants} />

- **Neutral** — everyday labels (plan tier, category, counts).
- **Brand** — feature highlights, "Most popular" pills.
- **Accent** — "Best value" / deal callouts. Pairs with violet only.
- **Success / Warning / Danger / Info** — system-feedback only.
- **AI match** — signature gradient, reserved for AI match surfaces.
- **Outline** — archived / de-emphasized labels.

## With dot

<Canvas of={BadgeStories.WithDot} />

For live-state indicators, prepend a 6px colored dot glyph (inherits text
color). Use sparingly — when "Live" / "Draft" / "Overdue" status is the
primary thing the reader needs to see.
